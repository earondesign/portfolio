ABOUT ME

Ever since I was a kid I've been highly creative, good at art and a problem solving, my favorite type of games were puzzles and strategy based video games. Until this day, I have the most fun when I'm ideating, strategizing and experimenting, which is what I love the most about applying UI and UX in product development. Every new project comes with a different set of challenges and learnings and I really enjoy the whole process of discoverability and finding new ways of connecting the user and technology.

Through UI, UX, and design thinking, I found an alignment in my career that truly satisfies my empathetic heart and creative nature. My mind is constantly looking to improve everything, even my own behavior, which has led me to study psychology, habit loops and semantic experience. With this mindset I am able to holistically continue to explore the delicacy of interactions between user and interface.
